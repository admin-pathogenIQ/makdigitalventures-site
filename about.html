About Mak Digital Ventures

Mak Digital Ventures is a U.S.-based digital product studio founded by Dr. Makafui Gbogbo. The company specializes in the design and development of scalable mobile applications and digital tools.

Headquartered in Ohio, Mak Digital Ventures focuses on building high-quality applications across healthcare technology, sports entertainment platforms, productivity tools, and financial management systems.
