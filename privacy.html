Privacy Policy

Last updated: May, 2026

Mak Digital Ventures builds and publishes mobile apps and websites. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our products.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: e.g., messages submitted through a support form or email.
  • App/website usage data (if enabled): basic analytics such as device type, pages viewed, and app events.
  • AI/chat features (if used): text you type into the chat may be processed to generate responses.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve app/website functionality
  • To respond to support requests
  • To troubleshoot bugs, performance, and security issues
  • To comply with legal requirements

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers (for example, hosting, analytics, or AI processing) only as needed to operate the product.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Our products are not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Permissions & Device Access

Camera & Photos: Our app may request access to your device camera or photo library only when you choose to upload or take a profile picture. This access is user-initiated and used solely for profile personalization.

Push Notifications

We may send notifications such as match updates, goals, or important app events. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

User Accounts

We use Firebase Authentication to manage user accounts. Information such as email address and user ID is stored securely to provide app functionality.

Data Storage

User data such as profile information, favorite teams, and notification preferences are stored securely using Firebase Firestore.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google policies.
